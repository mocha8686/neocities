## another blog forgotten

alright, i swear i have a good reason... kinda.

my other blog post, i posted right before finals, so i had to lock in hard for those, and then winter break came and i just lost all of my productivity, like my body just didn't want to do anything brain anymore. it's a problem i've had for a while now, where like once i come home for break, i just lose all my will to create, and instead i just rot on the 'puter playing my silly games and watching my silly youtubes. granted, i was also pretty busy for the holidays, but i was pretty free since january.

then, this semseter hit me with the most work i've ever had in all of my education. not only do i have a fairly full schedule with pretty large workloads for each class, but i've also decided to take on being a grader for a different class with the most unorganized professor, which, to be fair, it probably isn't his fault.

on top of all of that, i've become a research assistant for another professor because i was one of the few people in his class to get an A, so i guess that's pretty cool. even crazier though, this research topic is actually pretty prevalent in the field right now (ant colony optimization algorithms for instruction scheduling, parallelized to run on the GPU) and so my professor actually has ties to AMD since they have a whole ass team working on this exact problem, and furthermore, one of the papers this research group put out was recognized by MIT for having the potential to make a big change for the field in the near future, and one of the assistants was invited to give a talk there! and now i'm helping out with it too, so uh... yeah.

but anyways, i'm finally starting to get a grip on schoolwork, and since midterms are rolling up, i've got way more free time to myself and the motivation to do something with it other than doomscroll. hooray!
